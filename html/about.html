About Me

I'm a Quantum Computing and Machine Learning Engineer passionate about pushing the boundaries of quantum technologies and artificial intelligence. My work focuses on developing innovative solutions at the intersection of quantum computing and machine learning, particularly in areas like quantum generative models and high-energy physics simulations.

With a background in physics and computer science, I bring a unique perspective to solving complex computational challenges. I'm particularly interested in quantum machine learning applications in scientific computing and the development of quantum algorithms for simulation and optimization problems.

Currently, I'm focused on advancing the field of quantum generative adversarial networks (QGANs) and their applications in Monte Carlo simulations, particularly for high-energy physics experiments.
