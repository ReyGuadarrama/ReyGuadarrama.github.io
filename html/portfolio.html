QGANs for Monte Carlo Simulations

Implemented quantum generative adversarial networks for high-energy physics simulations. Developed a novel architecture using quantum circuits with auxiliary registers for generating ECAL and HCAL detector images simultaneously. Successfully replicated energy deposit patterns in both channels while preserving implicit data correlations.

Single Measurement Wave Union TDC

Developed a novel time-to-digital converter achieving 5.65 ps resolution using a subsampling-scheme-based codification. Implemented on Cyclone V FPGA for high-precision time measurements in particle physics experiments. Achieved superior linearity compared to traditional methods.
